# Privacy Policy for Recipe Minimalist

**Last Updated: October 2024**

Recipe Minimalist ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.


# Privacy Policy

### 1. Data Collection
We use Firebase Authentication to securely store your email and password. Your profile information and names are stored in Cloud Firestore.

### 2. Local Storage
We use SharedPreferences to store your profile photo and app preferences locally on your device for a faster experience.

### 3. AI Processing
When you scan your fridge ingredients, that data is processed by Google AI (Gemini) to generate recipes. No personal identity data is shared with the AI model.

### 4. Advertisements
We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers to provide relevant advertising.

### 5. Security
We prioritize your data security but remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
