# Privacy Policy for Recipe Minimalist

**Last Updated: October 2024**

Recipe Minimalist ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Information We Collect
* **Account Information:** We use Firebase Authentication to manage your account. We store your email and password securely via Google Firebase.
* **Profile Data:** Your first name, last name, and profile settings are stored in Google Cloud Firestore.
* **Local Data:** We use SharedPreferences to store your profile photo and user preferences locally on your device for performance.

### 2. AI Technology
Our app uses **Google AI (Gemini)** to generate recipes. When you scan your fridge or list ingredients:
* Ingredient data is sent to Google AI to process a recipe.
* We do **not** send your name, email, or any personal identity data to the AI model.

### 3. Advertisements
We display ads using **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising ID to show you relevant ads and for analytics.

### 4. Data Retention
You can delete your account at any time within the app settings. Deleting your account will remove your data from our Firebase database.

### 5. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
* Google Firebase (Authentication & Database)
* Google AI / Gemini (Recipe Generation)
* Google AdMob (Advertisements)
